Argentina Programa

This is a national and federal Argentine program that aims to train people in languages and knowledge about programming, testing and digital skills to enhance employability in the software and technology sector. The course is structured in two stages, #SéProgramar and #YoProgramo. The first one teaches the basic fundamentals of programming and the second aims to deepen the knowledge and form a junior full stack web profile.
